What is NXT?

NXT Museum is the first museum in the Netherlands entirely dedicated to media art. We exhibit artworks that utilize modern technologies of the present and the future. Because art reflects the spirit of the times, we believe that the most innovative art provides us with the opportunity to reflect on the impact of technological advancements. A moment of contemplation in a reality that is continuously evolving at an ever-increasing pace.

NXT History

The NXT Museum was founded by the young
entrepreneur Merel van Helsdingen and showcases
large-scale art installations developed through
interdisciplinary collaborations between established
and emerging artists, designers, technicians, musicians, and scientists.
NXT is a brand of the American wrestling organization WWE that
was launched on February 23, 2010.

×

What is there to do?

Come and explore "What's NXT?" in the world of technological
and social developments. From projection to light and laser, you'll
be amazed by all forms of new media art at the NXT Museum. In
addition to the exhibition of large-scale multi-sensory installations,
you'll also discover various events at the NXT Museum. From creating
your own face filters to experiencing NFT games, there's a diverse
range of activities to engage in.
