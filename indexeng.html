What is NXT?

NXT Museum is the first museum in the Netherlands entirely dedicated to media art. We exhibit artworks that utilize modern technologies of the present and the future. Because art reflects the spirit of the times, we believe that the most innovative art provides us with the opportunity to reflect on the impact of technological advancements. A moment of contemplation in a reality that is continuously evolving at an ever-increasing pace.

NXT history

The NXT Museum was founded by the young entrepreneur Merel van Helsdingen and showcases large-scale art installations developed through interdisciplinary collaborations between established and emerging artists, designers, technicians, musicians, and scientists.
